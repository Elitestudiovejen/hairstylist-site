Our Story

The studio
behind the look.

Maison Strandvær was built on a simple belief: that great hair is the result of great listening. We take the time to understand you before we touch a single strand.

Founded 2018

A small studio with big intentions — born from a love of craft and a conviction that every client deserves expert attention.

Maison Strandvær opened its doors in the autumn of 2018, tucked into a quiet street in Oslo. What started as a one-chair operation has grown into a full studio — but the ethos has never changed.

We believe the best haircut isn't the most dramatic one. It's the one that suits you so well you forget you're wearing it. We bring that same principle to colour, to styling, and to every conversation we have with a client.

Our stylists train continuously — at international shows, with visiting educators, and with each other. Great craft is a moving target, and we're always chasing it.
